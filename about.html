About Me

As a designer, I am interested in exploring novel 3D forms through computational methods and digital fabrication technology. Before starting my education in architecture, I was doing research in molecular structures. The experience in molecular modeling has greatly influenced my design approach. With my passion for both art and computer programming, I have developed scripts for generating forms and computational tools for bridging design and fabrication.
